Build instructions (TeX Live/MiKTeX):

1) pdflatex main.tex
2) bibtex main
3) pdflatex main.tex
4) pdflatex main.tex

Notes:
- No figures are required.
- If you submit to arXiv, include both `main.tex` and `references.bib` in the upload.
- Ensure `\bibliographystyle{plain}` and `\bibliography{references}` remain unchanged.
